The Haukai Restaurant

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands. It was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area.

It is located in the heart of Kerikeri and is a favourite from locals and tourists. The restaurant has a strong focus on maori culture and maori kai. The restaurant is currently only open in the evenings but in the future are hoping to be open all day.
