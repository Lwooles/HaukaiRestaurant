Privacy Statement

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • name
  • contact information
  • booking information

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • contact customers in regards to their bookings and to check for availability. The contact information provided allows us to contact you back with confirmation of bookings.

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter an email address:

  • we'll be unable to send a booking confirmation email.
  • we'll be unable to take a booking online as we need contact information

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at thehaukairestaurant@gmail.com, or 09 401 4019, or 1 Island Street, Kerikeri, Bay of Islands.
